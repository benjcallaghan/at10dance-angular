Home Classroom Attendance Manager @10Dance

@10Dance is an attendance application originally designed to help Sunday School teachers keep track of the students in their classes. To get started, click the menu icon and choose Setup. The Students tab will enable you to manage your students. Click on the Classes tab to create new classes and assign students to each class.

Once you have a database of classes and assigned students, access the class roster by selecting the class from the main menu. Select students to check them in or out of your class, and also to send a message to the students' parents.
